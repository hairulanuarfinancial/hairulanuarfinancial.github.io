Begin with a Conversation.

If what you’ve read here resonates with you, the next step does not need to be big.

Sometimes, one short conversation is enough. It usually takes around 15 minutes. No slides. No presentations. No pressure to make a decision.

The purpose is simply to create space — to understand your family’s situation and see whether this approach is suitable for you.

How It Works

It usually begins with a simple WhatsApp message. We set a suitable time, then speak in a calm and honest manner.

You share a little about your current situation and the questions on your mind. I listen, respond where helpful, and explain the next step only if it is relevant.

By the end of the conversation, we are both clear whether it makes sense to continue — or if the discussion alone is enough. Either is perfectly fine.
